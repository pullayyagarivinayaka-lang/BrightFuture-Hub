Step-by-step Roadmaps

Your exact journey, year by year

Select a path to see every step, exam, and decision point from 10th to your career.

🎓 10th Board Exams (SSC/CBSE/ICSE/State Board)

Age 15–16Milestone

Complete your 10th with focus on Maths and Science. Score above 60% to keep all options open. Above 75% opens competitive stream options. Check your state board results on official portals.

🔀 The Big Decision: What next after 10th?

After ResultsDecision Point

You have 3 main routes:

  • Intermediate (11th & 12th) — Opens degree colleges, MBBS, B.Tech, B.Pharm
  • Polytechnic Diploma — 3-year technical diploma, job-ready or lateral entry B.Tech
  • ITI — 1–2 year skill training, direct jobs in railways, factories, government

⚖️ Intermediate vs Polytechnic — Quick Comparison

✅ Choose Intermediate if…
  • You want MBBS, B.Tech, B.Pharm
  • You scored 70%+ in 10th
  • You can study 2 more years
  • You want NEET, JEE, EAMCET options
✅ Choose Polytechnic if…
  • You want a job in 3 years
  • You're interested in hands-on work
  • Family needs you to earn sooner
  • You can do lateral entry B.Tech later

📋 Group Selection in Intermediate

Admission TimeAction
  • MPC (Math, Physics, Chemistry) → B.Tech, Engineering, BCA
  • BiPC (Biology, Physics, Chemistry) → MBBS, B.Pharm, Nursing, Agriculture
  • MEC (Math, Economics, Commerce) → B.Com, CA, MBA, BBA
  • CEC (Commerce, Economics, Civics) → B.Com, Law, Arts, Finance

📝 Polytechnic Entrance Exam (POLYCET / DECE)

After 10th ResultsExam

TS POLYCET / AP POLYCET or state equivalent. Based on 10th Maths and Science. Score determines branch and college.

  • Branches: Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Electronics, Chemical
  • Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)
  • Government college fees: ₹15,000–₹30,000/year

📚 Polytechnic Years 1–3

3 YearsStudy + Internship
  • Year 1: Foundation subjects (Maths, Physics, Drawing)
  • Year 2: Core branch subjects + mini projects
  • Year 3: Advanced subjects + internship + main project

🔀 After Polytechnic — Two Strong Options

After DiplomaDecision
  • Get a Job directly — Technician/Junior Engineer in private sector (₹1.5–3 LPA)
  • LECET (AP) / TS ECET (TS) — Lateral entry to B.Tech 2nd year, complete B.Tech in 3 more years
  • APPSC/TSPSC Junior Engineer — Government JE posts (₹4–6 LPA)

📚 Class 11 — Choose Your Group

After 10thAdmission

Junior College (AP/TS) or Higher Secondary (other states). Key choice: MPC for engineering/science, BiPC for medical, MEC/CEC for commerce.

📝 Class 11 & 12 — Study + Entrance Prep

2 YearsStudy
  • MPC: Prepare for EAMCET / JEE Mains / JEE Advanced alongside board
  • BiPC: Prepare for NEET alongside board
  • CEC/MEC: Prepare for CA Foundation / CLAT / CUET

🎯 IPE (Intermediate Public Examination)

After 12thBoard Exam

Score 60%+ minimum for most degree admissions. Marks needed for MPC: 75%+ for top B.Tech colleges. BiPC: 60%+ for MBBS eligibility in NEET.

📝 EAMCET / JEE Mains

After 12thEntrance Exam
  • TS EAMCET — For Telangana state colleges
  • AP EAMCET — For AP state colleges
  • JEE Mains — For NITs, IIITs, CFTIs
  • JEE Advanced — For IITs (top ~2.5 lakh JEE Main qualifiers)
  • State-specific: MHT-CET (Maharashtra), KCET (Karnataka), TNEA (Tamil Nadu)

🏫 B.Tech — 4 Years

4 YearsDegree
  • Top branches: CSE → ₹5–25 LPA, ECE → ₹4–12 LPA, Mechanical → ₹3–8 LPA
  • Best colleges: IITs, NITs, JNTU, RGUKT (TS/AP)
  • Key: internships from 2nd year, projects, competitive coding

🚀 After B.Tech

Age 22+Career
  • Campus placements — TCS, Infosys, Wipro, HCL (mass recruitment)
  • Product companies — Google, Microsoft, Amazon (5–25+ LPA)
  • GATE exam → M.Tech / PSU jobs (BHEL, ONGC, BSNL)
  • GRE → MS abroad (USA, Germany, Canada)
  • MBA → IIM, IIT MBA (management roles)

🌿 BiPC Intermediate (Class 11–12)

2 YearsStudy

Biology, Physics, Chemistry. This opens MBBS, B.Pharm, D.Pharm, B.Sc Nursing, Agriculture, and more.

⚖️ D.Pharm vs B.Pharm

D.Pharm (Diploma)
  • 2 years after Intermediate
  • Can open own medical shop
  • Quick path to earning
  • Fees: ₹50k–₹80k total
B.Pharm (Degree)
  • 4 years after Intermediate
  • Research, drug development
  • Can do M.Pharm or PharmD
  • Higher salary potential

📝 EAMCET (Pharmacy Stream)

After 12thExam

AP/TS EAMCET. Government pharmacy colleges have fees as low as ₹15,000/year. Top companies in TS: Dr. Reddy's, Hetero, Aurobindo, Divi's.

🚀 After B.Pharm

CareerOptions
  • Pharma Industry (Production, QA, QC): ₹3–8 LPA
  • Medical Representative: ₹3–6 LPA + incentives
  • M.Pharm → GPAT exam → Specialization
  • Own Pharmacy / Medical Store
